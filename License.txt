The MOVES5 Release Candidate is made available for testing purposes 
and for modelers to become familiar with functional changes between 
MOVES4 and MOVES5 before MOVES5.0.0 is released. Emission results
of MOVES5.0.0 may differ from the results of this release candidate. 
Results from this version may not be used in work for state 
implementation plans, conformity determinations, or for any other
regulatory purpose.
